Core Features

Everything your clinic needs,
nothing it doesn't

Four powerful modules work together seamlessly so you can focus on what you do best — delivering exceptional client experiences.

📅

Calendar & Booking

A beautifully functional calendar with day, week, and month views. Clients book 24/7, staff see their schedules in real-time.

  • Drag-and-drop rescheduling
  • Real-time availability engine
  • Multi-staff, multi-location view
  • Embeddable booking widget
  • Deposit collection at booking
  • Automated SMS/email reminders
👤

Client Management

A full CRM built for service businesses. Every client has a profile, history, and communication log — all in one place.

  • 360° client profiles
  • Complete appointment history
  • Medical notes (HIPAA-encrypted)
  • Smart tags & segmentation
  • No-show tracking & scoring
  • Automated re-booking campaigns
💳

Payments & Billing

Stripe-powered payments built right into the booking flow. Collect deposits, process payments, and track revenue effortlessly.

  • Online deposit collection
  • Invoicing & receipts
  • Revenue analytics dashboard
  • Subscription management (Stripe)
  • Automatic refund processing
  • Multi-currency support
👥

Staff Management

Manage your whole team from one place. Set schedules, track performance, and let staff focus on clients instead of admin.

  • Individual staff calendars
  • Availability & leave management
  • Role-based access control
  • Performance & utilisation metrics
  • Staff-client assignment rules
  • Commission tracking
